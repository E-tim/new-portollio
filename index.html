QR Menu

I make a cool QR Menu for Hotels and Restaurants that is accessible for customers or lodgers on their phone after scanning the QR code. The QR Menu is easy for the hotels and Restaurants management to update within a minutes if there's any need to change the price or to remove a particular item , if it's not available at that moment.


Reasons why you need QR Menu

  1. It is easy to update menu items
  2. Unlike the menu card , QR menu saves time and money to print if there's need to change price
  3. Enhanced hygiene and safety by eliminating menu sharing between customers
  4. Reduce staff contact with customers
  5. Entice with food images

HEALTH TIPS

I used to eat whatever i want to eat and drink alcohol , I don't care if they are good for my health or not. I wouldn't go to gym , I ate late at night , sometimes wokeup in the middle of night just to have some bread or chocolate . One thing i noticed , I was always sick . I would go to hospital complaining . Dr , in the hospital never asked me about what I eat or if I am living a healthy life style . It was always about writing script to get expensive pain-killer and some anti-inflammatory medications at their pharmacy .

My life changed when I came accros AMERIX on twitter , He changed my life and I am sure alot of men out there too . I learnt from him you can't just eat anything because they are sweet , you aske yourself is this good for me , what are the side effect of eating this or drinking alcohol. I think first thing to healthy life is to know what you are putting in your mouth if it is good for you or not .


Let us quickly talk about four(4) important topic here. To know more about health , I will suggest you follow him on twitter @amerix

1. Food

2. Fasting

3. Gym (body)

4. 4Bs

1. Food : Before you eat anything , ask yourself is this good for me . The sugar , sodas, juice, energy drinks, alcohol and all different types of junk foods. If I take this , won't this lead to obesity, chronic inflamation & metabolic syndrome (hypertension, & diabete) . You will pick the best engine oil for your car , why can't you select a healthy food and drink.


2. Fasting : When we fast , fats store in the liver is converted to energy for body and brain to use . Research shows that we burning fat to survive . It is good to fast daily . When I started it was hard because I like eating atleast three times a day , breakfast, lunch and dinner with some snacks in between. With time and consistency I fast daily . Take your time and watch the video below to see why fasting is good for you.

3. Gym : The benefits of lifting weights include building muscle, burning body fat, strengthening your bones and joints, reducing injury risk, and improving heart health -source(google). Lifting heavy weight and and do some exercise is the best way to start my day . I used to carry 20kg and do 15 decline and incline push-up . That's too low , right ? yes I know that . right now, I lift 30kg and more and i do 40 push in 10 set. I see how that have made my bone strong and I like the shape of my body . Starting my day this way gives me confidence , and now, it's part of me and i can't do without it.


4. 4Bs : 4Bs of a good morning by @amerix are

1. Benediction : Pray, meditate, connect with your ancestors


2. Bed : Clean , lay your bed and if there's need to change the bedspread


3. Body: Go to gym and lift some heavy weight
